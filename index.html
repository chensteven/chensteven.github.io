Profile

As a Data Scientist with Air Miles, one of Canada's foremost loyalty programs, my role encompasses the leadership and development of sophisticated offer and product recommendation systems, as well as customer lifecycle models. These initiatives are integral to enhancing our marketing operations.

Previously, I held a pivotal role at Zero Gravity Labs, where I spearheaded the creation and development of advanced computer vision prototypes and proof-of-concepts. These were implemented on a variety of IoT devices, including Raspberry Pi, Jetson TX2, and tablets, with a focus on augmenting customer experiences in brick-and-mortar retail environments.

I'm also a part-time instructor and mentor at WecloudData teaching various topics like machine learning, computer vision, MLOps, and applied ML for business.

During my undergraduate studies, I gained diverse experience through internships at various companies, focusing on user interface design, web application development, and data analysis. I also did part-time research at various labs (see below).

My educational background is rooted in Seneca College and York University, where I pursued studies in web programming, cognitive science, and computer science.
